üí¨ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I donate clothes?

Find a nearby bin on our map, bring your clean clothes, and scan the QR code to record your donation.

What items can I donate?

We accept clean, gently-used clothing for men, women, and children. Shoes, bags, and accessories are also welcome!

How do eco-points work?

You earn 10 points per item donated. Points can be redeemed for rewards and unlock special badges.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! We provide donation receipts for tax purposes. Check your profile for your donation history.

Where do donated clothes go?

Clothes are sorted and distributed to local charities, shelters, and families in need. Some items are recycled into new materials.
